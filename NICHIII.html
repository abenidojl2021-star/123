NICHI

Nichi—

My Sun, My Day

You're such a wonderful existence I only dreamt of.
The way you smile, the way you laugh,
And the brilliance in those eyes that pierce through me.
I can't help but wonder, how can this person be real?
Strong, yet delicate.
Gentle, kind, and knows her worth.
Logical, sensitive, and compassionate.
Smart and talented.
Witty and humorous.
Broken and molded through the hardships and experiences of life.
A living embodiment of a superwoman.

My Nichi—

You are the sun that brings light to my day.
The laughter that brings joy to my heart.
The smile that I will always admire.
I will be forever grateful that I had the chance to meet you and experience how amazing of a person you are.
I will always cherish the times we were together, talking about anything, like it all made sense.
You made me realize how wonderful this world can be.
And how far it can be more wonderful being with you.

I love you, and I mean it.

A love so wonderful, it made me imagine a future with you.

A love that may transcend time.

A love so short, yet feels real and right.

I love you, from the very bottom of my heart.

My Nichi. My sun. My day.
